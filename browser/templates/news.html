Welcome to the new CMS Conditions DB service 2014-01-01

We are pleased to introduce you the service that hopefully will give an answer to all your Conditions-related needs during the second data taking period. The service has been built on top of new technologies and was made possible thanks to a major redesign to the Conditions database and software during the first long shutdown.

These are the main features we have put in place:

  • We have unified all the old services into one.
  • Accordingly, the User Interface now looks the same everywhere.
  • A single search form in the top gives you direct access to all the Conditions data: Tags, IOVs, Payloads, Global Tags, Records...
  • A menu that, when finalized, should be able to point you to anything you need.
  • An Upload Tag page, really easy to use, equivalent to the old upload.py script.
  • All the Global Tags' administration and workflows (i.e. the current GTC) will be integrated as well into this service.

Do not miss the Tutorial!

Feel free to drop suggestions or tell us about problems: use the Email the Web Experts button under the Support dropdown in the Menu.
